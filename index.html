3. Mandated Statutory Provisions
3.1 Salary & Insurance Escrow (24-Month Rule)

Under the 2026 AEWV framework, after the completion of **two (2) years of continuous service**, the Employer is mandated to facilitate all salary payments through a designated **Insurance Underwriter**. This ensures statutory protection of the Employee’s earnings and financial stability.

3.2 Medical Welfare & Annual Airfare

The Employer’s contracted **Medical Services Partner** is responsible for all annual health screenings. Furthermore, the Medical Provider is authorized to issue the **Annual Return Flight Ticket** (Economy Class) to the Employee's home country as part of the total welfare package.

3.3 Sustenance & Healthy Home Standards

The **Accommodation Management Provider** is responsible for ensuring the Employee’s residence meets NZ Healthy Home Standards. This includes the provision of **three (3) nutritional meals per day**, prepared according to NZ Food Safety guidelines.
