2. Role & Commencement

3. Compensation & Bank Dispersals

4. Comprehensive Corporate Benefits

4.1 Air Travel Logistics: The Employer will cover the full cost of International Air Travel for the Employee at the start and conclusion of the employment term.

4.2 Medical Care & Insurance: Full medical insurance coverage will be provided by the Employer, including emergency care and hospitalization. This is in addition to the standard ACC injury protection.

4.3 Accommodation Standard: The Employer provides Healthy Homes Compliant Housing. All utility expenses (Power, Water, Heat) are fully subsidized by the Employer.

4.4 Transport Provision: Dedicated shuttle transport between the residential accommodation and the workplace is provided daily at zero cost to the Employee.

5. Data Integrity & Privacy

5.1 Privacy Policy: Both parties are bound by the Privacy Act 2020. The Employer shall securely handle all client information. The Employee must maintain strict confidentiality regarding company trade secrets and intellectual property.

6. Health, Safety & Wellbeing

In accordance with the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015, the Employer is committed to a zero-harm workplace. The Employee must adhere to all safety protocols and use all provided protective equipment.

7. Statutory Leave Entitlements

The Employee is entitled to 4 weeks of Annual Leave and 10 days of Sick Leave per year, as per the 2026 NZ Employment Standards.

8. Resolution of Disputes

Any employment-related issue will be addressed in Good Faith. If a resolution cannot be reached internally, the parties will utilize the Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment (MBIE) mediation services.

9. Termination Framework

Either party may terminate this agreement with 4 weeks' written notice. Summary dismissal remains an option for the Employer in the event of serious misconduct (theft, fraud, or unsafe behavior).

10. Execution of Agreement

The Employee acknowledges they have been advised of their right to seek independent legal advice and has had sufficient time to do so before signing this document.
