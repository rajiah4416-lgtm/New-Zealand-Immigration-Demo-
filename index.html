Section 4: Comprehensive Company Benefits

4.1 Air Travel: The Employer provides fully funded international airfare (Economy Class) for the Employee from their country of origin to New Zealand at the commencement of this contract, and a return ticket upon completion.

4.2 Medical & Insurance: The Employer shall maintain a comprehensive health insurance policy for the Employee. This includes primary healthcare, hospital surgical cover, and accidental injury insurance through ACC and private providers.

4.3 Accommodation: The Employer provides subsidized, high-standard accommodation. This includes all utility costs (Water, Power, Heating) and complies with the Healthy Homes Standards 2026.

4.4 Transportation: The Employer provides dedicated daily transport between the Employee’s residence and the workplace at no cost to the Employee.

Section 5: Privacy & Conduct Policies

5.1 Data Privacy: The Employer and Employee shall adhere to the Privacy Act 2020. The Employer will protect the Employee's personal information, and the Employee shall protect all company trade secrets and client data.

5.2 Digital Security: Any use of company systems must comply with the Cybersecurity Policy. Unauthorized disclosure of company intellectual property is considered a breach of contract.

Section 6: Health, Safety & Leave

Both parties are bound by the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. The Employee is entitled to 4 weeks of annual leave and 10 days of sick leave as per the 2026 statutory requirements.

Section 7: Dispute Resolution (MBIE Standard)

In the event of an employment relationship problem, both parties agree to resolve the issue in Good Faith. If unresolved, the parties shall utilize the mediation services provided by Employment New Zealand (Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment). Personal grievances must be raised within the statutory period of 90 days.

Section 8: Termination of Agreement

This agreement may be terminated by either party giving 4 weeks' written notice. In cases of serious misconduct (theft, fraud, violence), the Employer may terminate this agreement without notice (Summary Dismissal) following a fair investigation process.

Section 9: Final Declaration & Execution

By signing this agreement, the Employee confirms they have read and understood all clauses, have been advised of their right to independent legal counsel, and accept the offer of employment voluntarily.
