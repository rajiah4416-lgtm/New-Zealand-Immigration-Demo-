3. Statutory Provisions (NZ Law)
3.1 Salary Protection & Insurance Underwriting

Upon the completion of **two (2) years (24 months)** of continuous service, the Employee’s salary payments will be processed via the Employer’s designated Insurance Company. This measure ensures financial security and income protection for the worker under the AEWV framework.

3.2 Medical Welfare & Annual Travel

In collaboration with the **Medical Services Provider**, the Employer ensures full health coverage. This medical company is responsible for the health clearance and the subsequent issuance of the **Annual Economy Class Air Ticket** to the Employee's home country.

3.3 Food & Living Standards

As part of the accommodation package, the **Accommodation Management Company** provides a fully catered service. This includes **three daily nutritional meals** compliant with NZ Food Safety standards, provided at the employee's place of residence.
