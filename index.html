4. Professional Conduct & Policy

The Employee agrees to adhere to the following Company Policies:

  • Uniform Code: The Employee is required to wear the official Company Uniform at all times during working hours to maintain professional standards.
  • Managerial Authority: The Employee must strictly follow the professional orders and instructions issued by their assigned Manager or Supervisor.
  • Privacy & Confidentiality: All internal company procedures, recipes, and guest information remain strictly confidential. Disclosure to third parties is grounds for termination.
  • Time Management: Punctuality is mandatory. Strict time limits for breaks and tasks must be observed as per the daily schedule.

5. Benefits & Provisions

Accommodation Free housing including utilities (Water/Electricity) provided by the Company.
Meals Three (3) complimentary meals provided daily during work shifts.
Air Travel One-way ticket from Country to Albania is provided at the start of the contract.
Health Insurance Full coverage under Albanian Social Security and Health Insurance (Sigurimet Shoqërore).

6. Annual Leave & Vacation

After completion of the first year of service, the Employee is entitled to Paid Annual Leave. For the upcoming vacation period, the Company will provide a One-Way flight ticket as part of the holiday benefit package.
