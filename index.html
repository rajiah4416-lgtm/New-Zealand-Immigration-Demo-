2. POSITION & COMMENCEMENT

2.1 Role: The Employee is appointed to the position of General Worker. The duties are as per the standard industry job description and Employer's requirements.

2.2 Joining Date: The Employee shall commence duties on 01/03/2026.

3. REMUNERATION & BANKING

3.1 Salary: The Employer agrees to pay a gross annual salary of NZD $0.00. Payment shall be made via direct electronic bank deposit into the Employee’s New Zealand bank account on a fortnightly basis.

3.2 Deductions: All payments are subject to PAYE Income Tax and ACC Levies as required by the New Zealand Inland Revenue Department (IRD).

4. COMPANY-PAID BENEFITS (FULL CARE)

4.1 Flight Tickets: The Employer shall provide one (1) return airfare ticket from the Employee’s country of birth to New Zealand at the start and completion of the contract term.

4.2 Medical & Health Insurance: The Employer will provide comprehensive medical insurance coverage for the duration of employment. This covers general practice visits, emergency care, and required prescriptions.

5. ACCOMMODATION & LIVING STANDARDS

5.1 Housing: The Employer agrees to provide suitable, safe, and healthy accommodation for the Employee. This accommodation meets the 2026 NZ Healthy Homes Standards. All utility costs (electricity, water, heating) are covered by the Employer.

5.2 Transport: The Employer will provide daily transport to and from the workplace and the provided accommodation at no cost to the Employee.

6. COMPANY PRIVACY & CLIENT DATA PROTECTION

6.1 Privacy Policy: The Employee agrees to abide by the Company’s Privacy Policy. You must protect all client records and business secrets. Unauthorized disclosure of company data is grounds for immediate termination.

6.2 Surveillance: In accordance with NZ law, the workplace may use CCTV for safety and security. All personal data collected will be handled according to the Privacy Act 2020.

7. HEALTH, SAFETY & WELLBEING

The Employer is responsible for providing a safe working environment under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. The Employee must follow all safety instructions and report any hazards immediately to the Health and Safety Representative.

8. LEAVE & HOLIDAYS

8.1 Annual Leave: 4 weeks’ paid leave per year. 8.2 Sick Leave: 10 days per year. 8.3 Public Holidays: Paid time off for all 11 NZ statutory holidays or time-and-a-half plus a compensatory day if worked.

9. TERMINATION OF AGREEMENT

9.1 Notice Period: Either party may terminate this agreement by providing 4 weeks' written notice. 9.2 Redundancy: The Employer will follow all NZ legal consultation processes in the event of restructuring.

9.3 Summary Dismissal: Immediate termination without notice applies in cases of serious misconduct, including theft, violence, or intoxication on duty.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION (MBIE)

If a problem arises, the parties will first try to resolve it in good faith. If unresolved, the Employee has the right to access free mediation services provided by Employment New Zealand (MBIE). Any personal grievance must be raised within 90 days.

11. FINAL DECLARATION

The Employee acknowledges they have been provided with a copy of this agreement, advised of their right to seek independent legal advice, and have accepted the terms voluntarily.
