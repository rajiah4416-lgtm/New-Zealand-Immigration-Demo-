6. COMPANY PROVIDED AMENITIES

As part of this international recruitment agreement, the Employer guarantees the following provisions at no cost to the Employee:

A. Accommodation

The Employer shall provide suitable, safe, and furnished accommodation within a reasonable distance from the workplace. This includes utility costs (electricity, water, heating) as per standard Irish residential norms.

B. Transportation

Free transportation will be provided by the company for the daily commute between the residence and the place of work. Furthermore, the Employer will provide the initial flight ticket to Ireland and a return ticket upon completion of the contract.

C. Medical Insurance

Full comprehensive medical insurance shall be maintained by the Company. The Employee will be registered with a local GP and covered for emergency hospital services under the Irish Health Service Executive (HSE) standards.

D. Food & Nutrition

The Employer will provide duty meals during working shifts. Outside of shifts, a food allowance or grocery provisions will be organized to ensure the Employee's well-being is maintained throughout the contract period.

7. ANNUAL LEAVE & PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
