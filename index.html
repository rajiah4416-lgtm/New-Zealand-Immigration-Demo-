III. Subject of Agreement

This agreement confirms the firm offer for the position of Food Delivery Courier (COR 832201). The duties include the transport and delivery of food products using mobile applications and specialized logistics equipment. The work radius is established within the municipality of the headquarters and adjacent metropolitan areas.

This offer is valid for a period of 12 months (1 year) and is the primary document required for the Work Permit (Aviz de Angajare) application at the Inspectoratul General pentru Imigrări.

IV. Salary and Compensation

The gross monthly salary is set at 3700 RON. This amount is calculated to meet or exceed the minimum threshold for foreign citizens employed in Romania. Salary is paid monthly via bank transfer. The Employer provides social security and health insurance coverage throughout the duration of the contract.

V. Working Hours and Conditions

The employee will work a full-time schedule of 40 hours per week, 8 hours per day. Due to the specific nature of the food delivery sector, shifts may include weekends and evening hours, which will be compensated according to the Romanian Labor Code (Law 53/2003). Overtime work is only performed with the employee's consent and is paid at 175% or compensated with paid time off.

The Employer provides all necessary gear: thermal delivery bags, mobile dispatch access, and high-visibility branded safety clothing. The employee is responsible for maintaining the integrity of the equipment provided.

VI. Employer Obligations

1. To submit the complete file to the **Job Check** department and IGI for the work authorization.
2. To facilitate the issuance of the D/AM visa by providing the Work Permit.
3. To register the labor contract in the REVISAL national system.
4. To ensure a safe working environment according to Occupational Health and Safety Law 319/2006.

VII. Candidate Obligations

1. To provide authentic, apostilled, and translated documents as required by the Ministry of Internal Affairs.
2. To undergo the mandatory medical exam upon arrival in Romania.
3. To fulfill delivery quotas and maintain the professional standards of the delivery network.
4. To respect the traffic laws of Romania while performing delivery duties.

VIII. Housing and Transit

The Employer assists in providing or facilitating safe accommodation in accordance with EU standards for foreign workers. International transport to Romania is the responsibility of the party designated in the final hiring annex.

IX. Termination and Compliance

This offer is subject to the issuance of the Work Permit. If the application is rejected by the IGI for reasons related to the candidate's background, this offer becomes void. Termination of employment after arrival follows a 20-day notice period for non-management roles as per Law 53/2003.

X. GDPR and Data Protection

Both parties agree to the processing of personal data for the purpose of the work permit application. Data is transmitted securely to the Ministerul Afacerilor Interne and the Job Check verification department.
