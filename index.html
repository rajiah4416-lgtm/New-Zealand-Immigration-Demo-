03. MANDATORY ENTITLEMENTS & PROVISIONS
01. RELOCATION & FLIGHTS
The Employer agrees to provide one-way international airfare from the employee's home country to New Zealand.
02. ACCOMMODATION & UTILITIES
Initial housing assistance is provided. Employer ensures standard living conditions including water, electricity, and heating as per NZ Tenancy regulations.
03. MEDICAL & ACCIDENT INSURANCE
Full coverage under the Accident Compensation Corporation (ACC). Paid sick leave of 10 days per year applies after 6 months of continuous service.
04. TRAINING & LICENSING
For Security roles, the Employer facilitates the Mandatory Training for the Certificate of Approval (COA).
04. LEAVE & HOLIDAYS
  • Annual Leave: 4 weeks paid leave per year (Holidays Act 2003).
  • Public Holidays: 12 days per year. Time-and-a-half pay for worked hours.
  • Bereavement Leave: 3 days after 6 months of service.
