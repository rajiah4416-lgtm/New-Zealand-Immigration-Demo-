2. Position & Term

The Employer offers the Employee the position of . This is a Fixed-Term Agreement for a duration of Three (3) Years (36 Months), specifically linked to the Accredited Employer Work Visa (AEWV) requirements.

3. Hours of Work

The Employee’s total number of guaranteed hours will be 40 hours per week. These hours will generally be worked Monday through Friday between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM, though flexibility is required for service roles.

4. Remuneration

The Employee will be paid an hourly rate of NZD $. This rate meets the current median wage requirements set by Immigration New Zealand for the AEWV scheme.

  • Payment Frequency: Fortnightly into a nominated NZ Bank Account.
  • Overtime: Any hours worked beyond 40 per week will be paid at the standard rate or as negotiated.
  • Taxation: PAYE (Pay As You Earn) tax will be deducted as per IRD regulations.

5. KiwiSaver

The Employer will comply with the KiwiSaver Act 2006. If the Employee is eligible, the Employer will contribute the required percentage of the Employee's gross salary (currently 3%) to their KiwiSaver fund.

6. Holidays & Leave Entitlements

The Employee is entitled to holidays and leave in accordance with the Holidays Act 2003.

Annual Leave4 weeks of paid annual holidays per year.
Public Holidays12 paid Public Holidays per year. If worked, pay is 1.5x plus a day in lieu.
Sick Leave10 days per year after 6 months of continuous service.
Bereavement Leave3 days for immediate family members.

7. Medical & Health Provisions

The Employer will provide comprehensive Medical Insurance coverage for the duration of the three-year term. This includes access to private specialists and diagnostic imaging to supplement the public ACC system.

ACC Coverage: All employees in NZ are covered by the Accident Compensation Corporation for work-related injuries. The Employer covers all ACC levies.

8. Accommodation & Living Provisions

The Employer provides high-quality, fully furnished accommodation for the Employee. This includes:

  • Private or semi-private bedroom in a shared modern residence.
  • All utilities (Water, Electricity, High-Speed Internet) paid by the Employer.
  • Proximity to local amenities and public transport links.

9. Transportation

Dedicated transportation will be provided for commuting between the residential address and the worksite. This is provided at no cost to the Employee.

10. Annual Air Travel

The Employer agrees to provide one return economy air ticket to the Employee’s home country for every 12 months of completed service. The initial ticket from the home country to NZ is also covered.

11. Health & Safety

Both parties will comply with their obligations under the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. The Employer will provide all necessary Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and safety training.

12. Confidentiality & Privacy

The Employee shall not disclose any confidential information regarding the Employer’s business. In accordance with the Privacy Act 2020, the Employer will securely store the Employee's personal data.

13. Termination of Employment

Either party may terminate this agreement by providing four (4) weeks' written notice. In cases of serious misconduct, the Employer may terminate without notice following a fair disciplinary process.

14. Employment Relationship Problems

If a problem arises, the parties will first attempt to resolve it through internal discussion. If unresolved, the services of the Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment (MBIE) mediation service may be sought.
