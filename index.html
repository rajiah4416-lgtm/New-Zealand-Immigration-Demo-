8. TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT

8.1 Notice Period: Either party may terminate this agreement by providing 4 weeks' written notice. The Employer reserves the right to provide payment in lieu of notice (GARDEN LEAVE).

8.2 Serious Misconduct: Instant dismissal without notice may occur in cases of theft, fraud, violence, or severe breach of company policy as defined in the Employee Handbook.

8.3 Redundancy: In the event of redundancy, the Employer will follow the consultation process required by the Employment Relations Act 2000.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

If an employment relationship problem arises, the following steps must be taken:

  • Step 1: Informal discussion with your immediate supervisor.
  • Step 2: Formal mediation via Employment New Zealand (MBIE).
  • Step 3: Application to the Employment Relations Authority (ERA).

Note: A personal grievance must be raised within 90 days of the action complained of occurring.
