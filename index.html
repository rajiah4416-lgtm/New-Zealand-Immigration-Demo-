3. Remuneration and Payment

The Employer shall pay the Employee an hourly rate of NZD $. This rate is compliant with the Median Wage thresholds set by New Zealand Immigration authorities.

  • Guaranteed Hours: 40 Hours per week.
  • Payment Method: Direct Bank Credit into a New Zealand bank account.
  • Taxation: Pay As You Earn (PAYE) will be deducted by the employer.

4. KiwiSaver Contributions

In accordance with the KiwiSaver Act 2006, the Employee will be enrolled in KiwiSaver if eligible. The Employer will contribute the statutory 3% (or current legal rate) of the Employee's gross salary to their nominated KiwiSaver provider.

5. Training and Development

The Employer will provide all site-specific and safety training required for the role. This includes professional development and compliance training related to New Zealand industry standards.

6. Leave Entitlements

The Employee is entitled to holidays and leave as per the Holidays Act 2003.

Annual Leave4 Weeks of paid leave per year.
Public Holidays12 Statutory days. Worked holidays paid at 1.5x + day in lieu.
Sick Leave10 Days paid leave per year (after 6 months service).
Bereavement3 Days for immediate family members.

7. Comprehensive Health Insurance

The Employer will provide a private medical insurance policy for the duration of the three-year term. This insurance covers specialized medical treatment and private hospital care, supplementing the New Zealand Accident Compensation Corporation (ACC) system.

9. Transport and Logistics

The Employer provides daily shuttle transportation from the residence to the workplace and return. This ensures the Employee's safety and punctuality as per the 3-year agreement.

10. Annual Home Leave

The Employer will fund one (1) economy class return flight to the Employee's home country every 12 months. The initial mobilization flight is also 100% employer-funded.

11. Health and Safety at Work

Both parties agree to uphold the Health and Safety at Work Act 2015. The Employer will provide all Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) including footwear, safety vests, and helmets where required by law.

12. Confidentiality and Privacy

All personal data is handled under the Privacy Act 2020. Information will only be shared with New Zealand Immigration (MBIE) and Inland Revenue (IRD) for legal compliance purposes.

13. Termination and Notice

Either party may terminate this agreement with 4 weeks' written notice. For instances of serious misconduct (as defined in the company policy handbook), summary dismissal may occur following New Zealand's legal "fair process" requirements.
